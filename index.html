Who Are We

Arcade Animal Aid is an effort to aid animals worldwide and allow those who cannot contribute directly to participate. We create enjoyable games that generate funds for charities such as Best Friend Animal Society and Animal Welfare Institute. All game profits are donated to these organizations. Our revenue is generated by the advertisements that are displayed within our games as well as through the purchases made within the game. We do not ask for direct donations.

Our Mission

Our platform serves as a nurturing environment for burgeoning game developers, offering comprehensive resources for learning, creating, and publishing games. Within our vibrant community, aspiring developers can not only showcase their talents but also forge valuable collaborations with peers, fostering a culture of innovation and growth. Moreover, we are committed to making a positive impact beyond the gaming sphere by directing a portion of the revenue generated from game sales towards supporting animal charities, aligning creativity with compassion and social responsibility.
