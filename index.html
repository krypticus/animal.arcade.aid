Who Are We

Arcade Animal Aid is an effort by Blur Labryth, an independent developer, to aid animals worldwide and allow those who cannot contribute directly to participate. We create enjoyable games that generate funds for charities such as Best Friend Animal Society and Animal Welfare Institute. All game profits are donated to these organizations Our revenue is generated by the advertisements that are displayed within our games as well as through the purchases made within the game. we do not ask for direct donations
